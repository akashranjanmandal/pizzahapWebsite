  Our Story

We Don't Just Make Pizza.
We Ignite Cravings.

PizzaHap was born in the heart of Uttarakhand — where the mountains are bold, the air is pure, and food is a celebration. We started with one obsession: to bring world-class pizza and burgers to every corner of the hills. No shortcuts. No compromise.

From Karanpriyag to Almora, our stores are where locals and travelers unite over a shared language: seriously good food. Every dough is hand-stretched, every sauce made fresh, every bite crafted to make you come back for more.
